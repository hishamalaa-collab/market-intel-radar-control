# Session End Reporting

## Purpose

Prevent knowledge loss and instruction drift at the end of a work session.

## Required Sections

1. Date.
2. Session objective.
3. What was completed.
4. What landed.
5. What failed or was rejected.
6. Locked decisions.
7. Open questions.
8. Pending tasks.
9. Files or locations touched.
10. Recommended next session start point.

Use `90_templates/session_report_template.md`.
