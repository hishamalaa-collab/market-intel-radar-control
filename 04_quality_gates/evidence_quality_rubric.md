# Evidence Quality Rubric

## Purpose

Rate how strong the evidence is before making recommendations or conclusions.

## Dimensions

Score each from 0 to 3:

1. Source reliability.
2. Source relevance.
3. Recency.
4. Specificity.
5. Cross-source confirmation.
6. Direct applicability.

## Score Bands

- 0–8: Low evidence.
- 9–13: Medium evidence.
- 14–18: High evidence.

## Use

If evidence is low, do not present conclusions as final. Present them as hypotheses or areas to verify.
