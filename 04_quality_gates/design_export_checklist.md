# Design Export Checklist

Use this before accepting design exports.

## Required Checks

- Correct dimensions.
- Correct page/card count.
- No contact sheet used as final output.
- No cropped cards.
- Brand furniture present on all required cards/pages.
- Approved copy preserved.
- No invented numbering, dates, locations, or extra labels.
- Typography follows the approved kit.
- Color rules preserved.
- Export format matches request.
- Each final file is independent when requested.

## Critical Warning

Do not upscale, slice, crop, or rebuild when the requirement is to export separate complete images from the approved design.
