# Project Boot — Market Intel Radar / Knowledge Studio

## Identity

Market Intel Radar is a project control and intelligence environment for converting external signals, source documents, reports, newsletters, client context, observations, and downloaded resources into reusable business thinking.

It is not a dead document archive.

## Core Principle

Documents are inputs.
Topics, reusable outputs, decisions, insights, proposal inputs, offer-design seeds, publishing inputs, and capability growth are the product.

## Two Streams

### Stream A — Market Intelligence / Outside-In

Inputs include:

- TMN emails.
- Training Industry emails.
- Newsletters.
- Reports.
- Links.
- Downloadable resources.
- Market observations.

Purpose:

- Convert external signals into live topics, insight notes, commercial angles, R&D seeds, and proposal intelligence.

### Stream B — Knowledge Development Studio / Inside-Out

Inputs include:

- Downloaded guides.
- Playbooks.
- Ebooks.
- Reports.
- Templates.
- Books.
- PDFs.

Purpose:

- Convert documents into topic clusters, reusable knowledge, frameworks, delivery assets, and internal capability.

## Correct Flow

```text
Google Drive
→ Source Documents Index
→ Topic Clusters
→ Live Knowledge Topics
→ Topic Pages
→ Reusable Outputs
```

## Operating Warning

Do not let Google Drive become the mental model of the system. Drive is storage. The topic layer is the product layer.
